The Little Prince
Novella by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

The story follows a young prince who visits various planets in space, including Earth, and addresses themes of loneliness, friendship, love, and loss.

Inside the book

Each chapter is an adventure!

Once when I was six years old I saw a magnificent picture in a book, called True Stories from Nature, about the primeval forest...

So I lived my life alone, without anyone that I could really talk to, until I had an accident with my plane in the Desert of Sahara, six years ago. Something was broken in my engine.

It took me a long time to learn where he came from. The little prince, who asked me so many questions, never seemed to hear the ones I asked him.

I had thus learned a second fact of great importance: this was that the planet the little prince came from was scarcely any larger than a house!
